TICKETS to visit convent
Book Your Individual Visit
The Convent can only be visited with a guide and by reservation.
For around 1:30 , let us guide you on a tour of our Convent from the inside. Discover the world of the Dominican friars and Le Corbusier's masterpiece. The guides will give the tour in French and/or English, depending on the visitor.
→ From SEPTEMBER to JUNE, GUIDED TOURS OF THE CONVENT take place
EVERY DAY from TUESDAY to SATURDAY at 2:30pm (with some exceptions, such as certain public holidays). Consult the schedule of sessions below.
SUNDAYS at 2:30 pm (in French and/or English) and at 3 pm (in French, from April to the end of June and from September to the end of October)
→ In JULY and AUGUST, 3 guided tours are offered each day
→ ONLINE RESERVATION ONLY via our box office below
The number of tickets is limited and reservations required CAN ONLY BE MADE ONLINE (secure payment by card) : €10 per person, €6 for under 26s, jobseekers & people with reduced mobility, free for under 12s.
Tickets (sent automatically to your e-mail address) are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged or refunded.
To access the interior spaces of the Convent, it is essential to be accompanied by a guide and to have booked your place online.
Roof terraces and cells are not open to visitors for safety and hygiene reasons.
All areas outside the convent are freely accessible from 8am to 7pm. The use of drones is not permitted. Please respect private areas and the peace and quiet of the site.
For further information, please use our contact form . We will get back to you as soon as possible. We look forward to welcoming you to our house.
Access Person with reduced mobility
Persons with reduced mobility have access only to the intellectual life floor (brothers' classrooms)
Access to the church require one able-bodied person to accompany you.
NOTRE COUVENT
Sainte-Marie de La Tourette
Est inscrit au patrimoine mondial de l’UNESCO depuis 2016. Propriété de l’ordre des Dominicains, il est la dernière grande oeuvre en France de l’architecte Le Corbusier.
