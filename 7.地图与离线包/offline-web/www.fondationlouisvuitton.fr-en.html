Gustave Fayet, Collector - Designer. Icons of Modern Art
From 09.10.2026 to 08.03.2027
Building on its “Icons of Modern Art” exhibitions, devoted to major collectors such as Sergei Shchukin, Mikhail and Ivan Morozov, and Samuel Courtauld, the Fondation continues its exploration of the pioneering figures who shaped the history of modern art with an exceptional exhibition: “Gustave Fayet: Collector - Designer”, on view from 9 October 2026 to 8 March 2027.
