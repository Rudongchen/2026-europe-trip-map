Musée d'Art Moderne de Paris
The museum is open from Tuesday to Sunday, from 10 am to 6 pm. On thursdays, the permanent collections are open until 9:30 pm.
The permanent collections are accessible without booking and free of charge. Please note that some rooms might be closed. We apologize for any inconvenience caused.

The Histories
