Safety
To ensure the safety of our visitors, the museum is strictly applying the security measures decided by the French authorities. Suitcases, travel bags and backpacks are not allowed in the museum, and cannot be left in the cloakroom. Please accept our apologies for the inconvenience and delay which may be caused by the extra security checks at the entrance.
